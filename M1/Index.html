Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and cremy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter

FEATURE IN ;

Fresch Cranberry Recipies That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For The Focaccia

  • 4 cups (500g)all purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teapons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukervarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra vergin oil, plus more for the pan

For The Topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, thougt it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cup fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour.
